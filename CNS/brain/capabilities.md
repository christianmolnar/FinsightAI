# FInsightAI Capabilities
*Core Financial Analysis and Trading Capabilities*

## Market Analysis Capabilities

### Technical Analysis
- **Chart Pattern Recognition**: Support/resistance, trend lines, head & shoulders, triangles
- **Momentum Indicators**: RSI, MACD, moving averages, Bollinger Bands
- **Volume Analysis**: Volume profile, accumulation/distribution, money flow
- **Market Timing**: Entry/exit signals, trend confirmation, reversal detection

### Fundamental Analysis
- **Financial Statement Analysis**: Balance sheet, income statement, cash flow analysis
- **Valuation Models**: DCF, P/E ratios, PEG ratios, enterprise value calculations
- **Sector Analysis**: Industry comparisons, competitive positioning, growth trends
- **Economic Indicators**: GDP, inflation, employment data, interest rate impacts

## Portfolio Management

### Portfolio Construction
- **Asset Allocation**: Strategic and tactical allocation across asset classes
- **Diversification**: Geographic, sector, and style diversification strategies
- **Risk Budgeting**: Risk allocation across positions and strategies
- **Rebalancing**: Systematic rebalancing rules and triggers

### Risk Management
- **Position Sizing**: Kelly criterion, fixed fractional, volatility-based sizing
- **Stop Loss Management**: Technical and fundamental stop loss strategies
- **Correlation Analysis**: Cross-asset correlation monitoring and adjustment
- **Scenario Analysis**: Stress testing under various market conditions

## Trading Execution

### Order Management
- **Order Types**: Market, limit, stop, stop-limit, trailing stop orders
- **Execution Strategy**: TWAP, VWAP, implementation shortfall algorithms
- **Slippage Management**: Optimal timing and order size determination
- **Cost Analysis**: Trading cost analysis and optimization

### Strategy Implementation
- **Systematic Trading**: Rule-based strategy execution and monitoring
- **Discretionary Support**: Research and analysis for manual decisions
- **Options Strategies**: Covered calls, protective puts, spreads, collars
- **Crypto Trading**: Digital asset analysis and portfolio integration

## Research and Analysis

### Market Research
- **Company Research**: Earnings analysis, management assessment, competitive analysis
- **Sector Studies**: Industry trends, disruption analysis, growth opportunities
- **Macroeconomic Analysis**: Policy impacts, currency effects, global trends
- **Sentiment Analysis**: Market sentiment indicators and contrarian signals

### Performance Analysis
- **Attribution Analysis**: Performance attribution across factors and decisions
- **Benchmark Comparison**: Relative performance measurement and analysis
- **Risk-Adjusted Returns**: Sharpe ratio, Sortino ratio, maximum drawdown analysis
- **Strategy Effectiveness**: Systematic review of trading strategies and outcomes
