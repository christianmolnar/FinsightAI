# FInsightAI Mandatory Checks
*Required Validation and Safety Protocols*

## Pre-Trade Mandatory Checks

### Risk Assessment Requirements
- [ ] **Position Size Validation**: Ensure position size does not exceed 5% of total portfolio
- [ ] **Concentration Check**: Verify single asset exposure does not exceed 10% of holdings
- [ ] **Correlation Analysis**: Check correlation with existing positions to avoid concentration
- [ ] **Liquidity Assessment**: Confirm sufficient liquidity for position entry and exit
- [ ] **Stop Loss Definition**: Mandatory stop loss level defined before position entry

### Compliance Validations
- [ ] **Regulatory Compliance**: Verify trade complies with applicable regulations
- [ ] **Account Suitability**: Confirm trade is suitable for account type and objectives
- [ ] **Documentation**: Ensure all trade rationale is documented
- [ ] **Approval Status**: Verify proper approval levels for trade size and type
- [ ] **Time Restrictions**: Check for trading window restrictions or blackout periods

### Data Quality Checks
- [ ] **Data Freshness**: Confirm market data is current and not stale
- [ ] **Source Verification**: Validate data sources are reliable and authorized
- [ ] **Price Reasonableness**: Check prices are within reasonable ranges
- [ ] **Volume Validation**: Ensure sufficient volume for execution
- [ ] **Market Status**: Verify markets are open and trading normally

## Post-Trade Mandatory Reviews

### Execution Validation
- [ ] **Fill Confirmation**: Verify trade executed as intended
- [ ] **Price Analysis**: Confirm execution price within acceptable range
- [ ] **Cost Assessment**: Review transaction costs and slippage
- [ ] **Position Update**: Update portfolio positions and exposures
- [ ] **Risk Recalculation**: Recalculate portfolio risk metrics

### Performance Tracking
- [ ] **Trade Documentation**: Document trade execution details
- [ ] **P&L Calculation**: Calculate immediate profit/loss impact
- [ ] **Attribution Analysis**: Attribute performance to specific factors
- [ ] **Benchmark Comparison**: Compare execution to relevant benchmarks
- [ ] **Learning Documentation**: Record lessons learned from trade

## Daily Mandatory Procedures

### Market Opening Checks
- [ ] **Portfolio Valuation**: Update portfolio valuations with current prices
- [ ] **Risk Monitoring**: Review overnight risk exposure changes
- [ ] **News Review**: Scan for overnight news affecting positions
- [ ] **Calendar Events**: Check economic calendar for market-moving events
- [ ] **System Status**: Verify all trading systems and data feeds operational

### Market Closing Procedures
- [ ] **Position Reconciliation**: Reconcile all positions and balances
- [ ] **P&L Calculation**: Calculate daily profit and loss
- [ ] **Risk Assessment**: Assess overnight risk exposure
- [ ] **Stop Loss Review**: Review and adjust stop loss levels as needed
- [ ] **Performance Recording**: Record daily performance metrics

## Weekly Mandatory Reviews

### Portfolio Assessment
- [ ] **Performance Review**: Comprehensive weekly performance analysis
- [ ] **Risk Analysis**: Deep dive into portfolio risk exposures
- [ ] **Strategy Effectiveness**: Evaluate trading strategy performance
- [ ] **Correlation Analysis**: Review correlations between positions
- [ ] **Rebalancing Assessment**: Determine if rebalancing is needed

### System Maintenance
- [ ] **Data Quality Audit**: Review data quality and accuracy
- [ ] **Model Performance**: Assess predictive model effectiveness
- [ ] **Alert System Check**: Verify alert systems functioning properly
- [ ] **Backup Verification**: Confirm data backups are current
- [ ] **Documentation Update**: Update strategy and procedure documentation

## Monthly Mandatory Assessments

### Comprehensive Review
- [ ] **Strategy Performance**: Comprehensive strategy performance review
- [ ] **Risk Model Validation**: Validate and update risk models
- [ ] **Client Reporting**: Prepare monthly client performance reports
- [ ] **Regulatory Review**: Review compliance with regulatory requirements
- [ ] **System Optimization**: Assess and optimize system performance

### Learning Integration
- [ ] **Pattern Analysis**: Analyze trading patterns and outcomes
- [ ] **Strategy Refinement**: Refine strategies based on performance
- [ ] **Knowledge Updates**: Update knowledge base with new insights
- [ ] **Skill Assessment**: Assess and improve analytical capabilities
- [ ] **Relationship Review**: Review collaboration effectiveness with other agents

## Emergency Protocols

### Market Crisis Response
- [ ] **Position Assessment**: Immediately assess all position exposures
- [ ] **Risk Calculation**: Calculate potential losses under stress scenarios
- [ ] **Liquidity Check**: Verify liquidity of all positions
- [ ] **Client Communication**: Prepare client communication plan
- [ ] **Escalation Protocol**: Escalate to Orchestrator for crisis management

### System Failure Response
- [ ] **Manual Backup**: Switch to manual trading procedures
- [ ] **Data Verification**: Verify data integrity and completeness
- [ ] **Communication**: Notify team of system status
- [ ] **Recovery Plan**: Implement system recovery procedures
- [ ] **Learning Documentation**: Document failure causes and responses

## Violation Consequences

### Minor Violations
- **Documentation**: Require detailed violation report
- **Review Process**: Mandatory review with Orchestrator
- **Corrective Action**: Implement specific corrective measures
- **Monitoring**: Increased monitoring for specified period

### Major Violations
- **Trading Suspension**: Temporary suspension of trading activities
- **Full Investigation**: Comprehensive investigation of violation
- **System Review**: Complete review of systems and procedures
- **Remediation Plan**: Formal remediation and improvement plan

---
*These mandatory checks ensure FInsightAI operates safely, compliantly, and effectively while maintaining the highest standards of risk management and fiduciary responsibility.*
